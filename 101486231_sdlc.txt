DevOps is important as it bridges the gap between development and operations. It promotes faster delivery, improved 
collaboration, and better-quality software.
~
One important thing learning DevOps is that Collaboration between teams, fostered by DevOps, eliminates silos and 
promotes better communication throughout the SDLC.
~
DevOps emphasizes the value of delivering smaller, incremental updates, which minimizes the risk of large-scale 
failures. The use of monitoring tools in DevOps helps maintain software performance and uptime, ensuring customer 
satisfaction. DevOps practices, such as automated testing, ensure that software remains stable even as new features 
are added.
~
