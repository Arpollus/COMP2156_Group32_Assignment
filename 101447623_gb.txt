### Fun Fact
1.  The St. James Campus is housed in the former Christie, Brown and Company Biscuit Factory, later a Hallmark Cards factory, integrating the college into Toronto's downtown core.

2. In 2022, George Brown College ranked among Canada's top 10 research colleges, placing 8th for overall research income and 4th for both the number of paid students and completed projects.

3. A combined $18 million donation, the largest in Ontario college history, was made to support the construction of Limberlost Place, a new addition to the Waterfront Campus.
